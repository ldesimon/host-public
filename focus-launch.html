Project Overview

Our path from here to a successful launch. Training and support will be provided at every step — no one is expected to figure this out alone.

Spring 2026

System Setup & Configuration

Focus is being configured for KIPP Miami — setting up school structures, enrollment, course catalogs, attendance codes, grading scales, and compliance rules. The data team and our implementation partners are handling the technical setup behind the scenes.

In Progress
Spring 2026

Data Migration & Validation

Student records, enrollment history, transcripts, grades, and Special Education data will be migrated from PowerSchool into Focus. The data team will run multiple validation passes to ensure everything transferred accurately. School staff may be asked to verify sample student records.

In Progress
Before Go-Live

Staff Training

Role-specific training will roll out in stages — operations and admin staff first, then teachers. Training will be hands-on with a sandbox environment where you can practice without affecting real data. Quick-reference guides and video walkthroughs will be available. By the first day of school, all staff will be equipped and ready.

Upcoming
Summer 2026

Go-Live

Focus becomes the live platform for KIPP Miami for the 2026-27 school year. All student records, grades, attendance, scheduling, and parent access will run through Focus from this point forward.

Target
August 2026

First Day of School

By the time students and families arrive, Focus will be fully operational. Training refreshers will be available in the first weeks of school, and support channels will be active to handle any questions quickly.

Target
Fall 2026

First State Reporting Milestone

The first FTE Survey submission to the Florida Department of Education using Focus. This confirms that enrollment data, attendance, and student information are reporting correctly in the new platform. The data and compliance teams will be monitoring this closely.

What's Changing For Me?

Select your role to see what stays the same, what's new, and what support you'll get.

For Teachers

Gradebook, assessments, attendance, and student culture — aligned in one place.

What Stays the Same

✓You still take attendance daily — same process, new interface
✓Your curriculum materials, lesson plans, and instructional tools don't change
✓Instructional tech apps stay the same — Clever, i-Ready, Amplify, mClass, and others are not changing
✓Your gradebook workflow stays familiar — daily entries, categories, and weighting

What's New & Better

★One login for grades, attendance, student info, assessments, and parent messaging — no more switching between PowerSchool, DeansList, and Illuminate
★Full mobile app — take attendance, enter grades, message parents, and give positive behavior awards from your phone
★Built-in assessment creator that matches FAST item types — create practice tests aligned to state standards in minutes
★Messages to families auto-translate with multiple language options and two-way reply
Training: Hands-on, role-specific training will be provided before go-live. All teachers will be prepared and supported by the first day of school.

For School Ops & Registrars

Less manual double-entry. A platform built for our needs that we own and manage.

What Stays the Same

✓Your core responsibilities — enrollment, attendance, scheduling, student records, reporting
✓State reporting deadlines and FTE submission requirements
✓Working with the data team on compliance questions

What's New & Better

★State reporting validation built into daily workflows — errors caught at point of entry, not at submission time
★Course Code Directory syncs nightly with FLDOE — no manual updates when courses change
★Focus Transfer: students coming from other FL districts auto-import their records, test history, transcripts, and immunizations in real time
★Master schedule builder automates period/section optimization with drag-and-drop adjustment
★FTE simulation reports let you check your data before submitting to the state
Training: Dedicated hands-on training covering enrollment, scheduling, attendance, and state reporting. Ops staff will be among the first trained.

For School Leaders & APs

Key protocols aligned so you can focus on what matters — students and assessments.

What Stays the Same

✓Your role in monitoring attendance, discipline, culture, and academic progress
✓Existing academic programs and curriculum (Fishtank, Illustrative Math, etc.)
✓Instructional tech apps stay the same — Clever, i-Ready, Amplify, mClass, and others are not changing
✓Support from the data team and ops team for reporting and compliance

What's New & Better

★Electronic discipline referral workflow — teacher to admin, with automated notifications, SESIR compliance, and equitable enforcement rules built in
★Built-in assessment tools aligned to FAST — create, administer, and review practice assessments directly in the platform
★Real-time school grade component tracking: learning gains, L25/L30, graduation rate, acceleration — all pre-calculated using exact state formulas
★Positive behaviors system with parent push notifications — culture tracking without a separate platform
★Impersonate any user role for troubleshooting — see exactly what a teacher or parent sees
Training: Targeted sessions on discipline configuration, assessment tools, and school-level reports. Details coming soon.

For Special Education Coordinators

A platform built to support our students — IEPs, 504s, and services, integrated into the student record.

What Stays the Same

✓Your compliance obligations — IEP timelines, manifestation determinations, FTE reporting
✓Collaboration with school teams on student services
✓State assessment accommodations and waivers remain in effect

What's New & Better

★Special Student Services (SSS) module: IEP, 504, ELL, MTSS, threat assessment, and health — all in one interface
★Automatic suspension limit alerts — configurable warnings when approaching manifestation determination threshold
★E-signature capture for IEP meetings — parents can sign from their phone or during in-person meetings
★Caseload management with progress monitoring alerts tied to report card posting windows
★Medicaid billing data capture — log services, times, and codes for export to billing
Training: Dedicated deep-dive with Focus Special Education specialists, separate from general staff training.

For Parents & Families

One app to stay connected to your child's education.

What Stays the Same

✓Access to your child's grades, attendance, and school information online
✓Communication from teachers and the school about your child
✓The ability to reach your child's school directly with questions

What's New & Better

★One app and one portal for everything — grades, attendance, discipline, positive behaviors, and school announcements
★Messages from school arrive in your language — and you can reply in your language too
★Push notifications when your child earns positive behavior awards
★E-sign documents like IEP plans from your phone — no need to come into school for every signature
★School message boards with flyers, announcements, and classroom updates — right in the app
Getting started: Download the Focus Community App (available on iPhone and Android) and set up your parent account. Instructions will be sent home before the school year begins.
